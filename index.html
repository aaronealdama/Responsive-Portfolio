About Me

My name is Aaron Ealdama and I am a current student at the University of Texas Coding Bootcamp. I was born in the state of Michigan and lived in the city of Detroit for 11 years before moving to Texas. I studied engineering at the University of Houston before realizing that my passion was in coding. My dream is to complete the Coding Bootcamp and start my career into freelance web design and mobile app developement.

The technologies I know so far include HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript ES6, jQuery, and Bootsrap for responsive design. Technologies I am excited to learn and explore include MySQL and Node for serverside development as well as popular JavaScript frameworks such as React and Angular. My dream is to one day create my own web development small business and bring my clients ideas to life. My slogan: If you can dream it, we can build it (I know, I'm working on it).
